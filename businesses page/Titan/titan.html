DESCRIPTION

The Titan Company Limited (Titan) Journey began with small but confident steps in 1984 as a joint venture between the TATA Group and Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). Started with an objective to create a Titan watch for everyone has now grown to be the fifth largest integrated watch manufacturer in the world which was only the beginning of a success story.
